How Does it Work?

 Scanning  Products

Scanning products allows you to gain a quicker way to dispose of your products. First, once you scan the product, it provides you with three features. With the first feature, it allows you to learn about the cautions of the product- such as warnings about various parts of the packaging. Also, allows you to learn about the type of plastic and its effects on the planet.

Help solve the global waste crisis.

We live in a consumerist society with products being able to appear within minutes of ordering them online. The app intends to be the little conscience you have at the back of your head and understand the effects of what happens when you purchase a specific product. The slight reminder of what you’re actually purchasing and its effects on the world.

Help tackle waste digitally for a cleaner future
